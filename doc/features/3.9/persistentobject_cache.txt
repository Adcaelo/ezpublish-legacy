*Title: Caching of eZPersistentObject.

*Documentation:

Caches eZPersistentObjects fetched from database in memory.

* New settings:

New INI settings in site.ini[PersistentObjectSettings] have been added:

- CachePersistentObject
  Set to true to enable caching of eZPersistentObject.

- PersistentObjectCount
  Number of eZPersistentObject to keep in memory.
